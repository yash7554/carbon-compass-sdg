Methodology

Emission factors are approximate, illustrative averages compiled from publicly available references (Our World in Data, UNEP Emissions Gap Report, EDGAR/JRC, IEA, UK DEFRA conversion factors) — not a certified carbon audit. Reference lines: India per-capita average ≈ 2.2 t CO₂/yr, global per-capita GHG average ≈ 6.4 t CO₂e/yr, 1.5°C-aligned individual target ≈ 2.3 t CO₂e/yr by 2030. All calculations run locally in your browser; no data leaves this device.
